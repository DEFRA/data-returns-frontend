Send landfill data returns

Use this service to send data returns to the Environment Agency (England only) about landfill compliance monitoring.

Report schedule 5 and 6 events directly to your local regulatory team or allocated Site Inspector.

Start now

Before you start

Read guidance on how to format landfill data returns (opens in a new window) to meet Environment Agency (EA) requirements, and download controlled lists of standard terms for some of your data.

To use this service you must:

  • follow EA formatting rules
  • save your data as a CSV file
  • give your email address for confirmation that you’ve sent your data
Warning To use this service you must be the permit holder or act on their behalf. When you send your data returns file you’re agreeing that — to the best of your knowledge and belief — the data you’ve submitted is true and not false or misleading.
